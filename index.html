Anyone can list "Power BI" and "Financial Modelling" on a resume. I'd rather just show you. Here's a complete financial analysis of Woolworths Group (ASX: WOW) — built with real data, real insights, and the kind of thinking I bring to every role.

I picked Woolworths deliberately — not a textbook case study, but a real ASX-listed company with messy FY2024 numbers (53-week year, $1.7B in non-cash impairments). The kind of data that breaks if you just plug it into a template. That's the point.

The analysis covers FY2022–FY2025 actuals with FY2026E projections across three scenarios — because any analyst can report what happened, but the useful ones tell you what might happen next.
